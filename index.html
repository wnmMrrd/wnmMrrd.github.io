About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), supervised by Prof. Man Ho Allen Au. Before joining PolyU, I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree from the ACM Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

My research focuses on threshold signature schemes and zero-knowledge proofs (ZKPs). Currently, I mainly work on the threshold BBS+ protocols and VOLE-based ZKP systems.
